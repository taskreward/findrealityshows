FAQ

How do I earn?

Primarily by using your new skills for freelance or jobs. A simple, optional referral program exists for sharing SkillMint—earn a small commission when friends enroll. No multi‑level structures.

Do you issue certificates?

Yes—every course includes projects and an optional final assessment. Pass it to unlock a shareable certificate link.

Is my data safe?

We store only what’s needed for your learning progress. You can export or delete your data at any time from the dashboard.

Refund policy?

14‑day no‑questions refund on Starter/Pro if you haven’t finished more than 20% of any course.
